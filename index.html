Our Story

Built on a Simple but Powerful Observation

The Problem

Surat's textile printing hubs consume thousands of tonnes of sublimation transfer paper daily. Used once, these sheets are discarded — sent back to paper mills where enormous energy is consumed to recycle them from scratch.

The Idea

Mr. Rajesh Yadav, a pioneer of digital sublimation printing in Surat, asked: what if used paper could find a second purpose without being ground back to pulp? He partnered with Mr. Kartik Singhal to build THE PRINT EMPIRE.

Our Mission

To transform textile printing paper waste into meaningful, sustainable value — minimizing energy consumption, reducing environmental impact, and building a greener future for India's textile and packaging ecosystem.
